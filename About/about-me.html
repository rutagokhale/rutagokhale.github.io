ABOUT ME

Hey there! I am Ruta Gokhale. I like chocolate chip cookies, morning hikes, making art, and lazy afternoons!
I am a User Experience Designer - what that means is, I solve different kinds of problems! I spend most of my time talking to a lot of people, asking many questions, sketching ideas, and ultimately making sure the problem has an effective solution.

I believe design is a process, not a result. It is a mindset that helps you find the right solution to the right problem. After working for 4+ years in the enterprise software space, I've come to realize, problems come with ambiguities and their fair share of constraints. And therefore, I think two things are most important to the craft of UX - empathy for the user and exploring creativityÂ within  the box.

Most recently, I worked at Collibra, designing experiences for a SaaS offering that enables privacy teams to create policies that protect sensitive information in an organization and optimize access management. Previously, I have worked at Thomson Reuters and Menlo Innovations designing web-based enterprise software and workflows.

I have always thought of myself as a student of science with a passion for art. So when I stumbled upon the field of Design, I had to go for it! I completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I then pursued a Master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor (Go Blue!). Today, I consider myself lucky to work in a role where engineering, design, and people converge.

If you want to chat about design, or simply get in touch, reach out to me via LinkedIn or email (ruta.gokhale6@gmail.com). I am always down for a cup of coffee or a good conversation!
