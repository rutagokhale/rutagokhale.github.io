DATA VISUALIZATIONS

I stopped eating cereal and here’s why…

I did a data-visual investigation into the health and nutrition of ready-to-eat breakfast cereals to create awareness about how unhealthy cereals really are.

The data is in the art!

I asked my friends to fill out a survey. I created 3 different visualizations, each designed to focus on a specific aspect of the collected data.

Visualization of The Office episodes

I visualized a text dataset using Python and R code, to create a visual interpretation of episodes from The Office TV show.

Week of food encounters

I illustrated my diet for a week through hand drawn patterns, inspired by Giorgia Lupi's 'Dear Data' project. I realized my meal times were often late but consistent, and I was consuming a good portion of processed foods each day.

Lotka Volterra equations

I created animated graphs using the JS d3 library to explain the food cycle and population patterns in nature using storytelling.
